Client's Settings
2012/05/10
 
Configure for your Mail Client on your PC. This example shows with Windows Live Mail.
[1] Start Windows Live mail and move to 'Account' tab and click 'Mail' button.
[2] Input email address, account's password, sender's name and check a box 'Configure Manually' and go next.
[3] Select IMAP or POP. This example selects IMAP. And input other information of your Mail server. Don't forget to check a box 'this server requires to authenticate' at the bottom.
[4] Click 'Finish'.
[5] Connect and get server's folder settings automatically.
