Services
2015/01/13
[1] If some services are unnecessary for your server usage, it had better to disable them. Make sure current services setting like follows.
[root@dlp ~]#
chkconfig --list | less

[2] For example, stop netfs and disable auto-start on system boot.
[root@dlp ~]#
/etc/rc.d/init.d/netfs stop

[root@dlp ~]#
chkconfig netfs off
